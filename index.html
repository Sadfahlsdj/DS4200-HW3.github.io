About Me

Hello! My name is William, and I’m passionate about data science. I created this website to share my journey and experiences with others who might have similar interests. Over the years, I’ve developed a deep appreciation for data mining and machine learning, and I’m excited to showcase some of my work and thoughts here.

I’ve always been fascinated by how machine learning is quickly evolving in today's ecosystem. Whether it’s excelling in classwork, winning hackathons, or pouring my heart into personal projects, I find joy in the process of creating and learning. This website is a space where I can document my progress, share my insights, and connect with like-minded individuals.

In my free time, I enjoy chess, piano, and gaming. I hope this website inspires you to explore your own passions and maybe even try something new!
